# Ampeachy's Mango Muffins

**Co-created with AI** on January 9, 2026| Documented February 22, 2026

## Ingredients (makes 12)
- 1–1¼ cups mango
- 1 tbsp water (if fresh)
- 2 tsp cinnamon (divided)
- 2 cups flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp salt
- 2 eggs
- ½ cup melted butter
- ¾ cup sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tsp vanilla
- Cinnamon sugar for topping

## Instructions
1. Cook mango with water and 1 tsp cinnamon for 5–7 min. Let cool.
2. Whisk flour, baking powder, salt, and 1 tsp cinnamon.
3. In another bowl, whisk eggs, butter, sugar, milk, and vanilla.
4. Pour wet into dry and stir until just combined.
5. Add extras if desired (coconut, walnuts, pineapple).
6. Fold in mango.
7. Fill muffin cups ¾ full. Top with cinnamon sugar.
8. Bake 375°F for 16–20 min.
